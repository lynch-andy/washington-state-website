City Information

Demographic Information

Olympia, with a population of approximately 52,000 residents, is a vibrant urban center with a median age of 35 years. The city's diverse population includes significant White, Hispanic, and Asian communities, fostering a multicultural environment.

Economic Information

The economy of Olympia is anchored by government services, healthcare, and education sectors. The city benefits from its status as the state capital, with numerous public sector jobs. The unemployment rate is around 4%, and the median household income is $60,000.

Geographic Information

Located at the southern end of Puget Sound, Olympia enjoys a picturesque setting with easy access to both coastal and mountain landscapes. The city's climate is characterized by mild, wet winters and dry, warm summers, making it ideal for year-round outdoor activities.
