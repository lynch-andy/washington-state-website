State Information

Demographic Information

Washington State is home to approximately 7.6 million people. The state boasts a diverse population with a median age of 37.5 years. Major ethnic groups include White, Hispanic, Asian, and African American communities, contributing to a rich cultural tapestry.

Economic Information

Washington’s economy is robust and dynamic, driven by industries such as technology, aerospace, agriculture, and trade. The state is home to globally renowned companies like Microsoft and Amazon, making it a hub for innovation. The unemployment rate stands around 4.5%, with a median household income of $78,687.

Geographic Information

Located in the Pacific Northwest, Washington State features diverse landscapes ranging from the rugged Pacific coastline to the majestic Cascade Mountains and the fertile Columbia River Plateau. The state enjoys a mild climate with wet winters and dry summers, ideal for outdoor activities year-round.
