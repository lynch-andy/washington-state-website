City Information

Demographic Information

Tacoma is home to approximately 219,000 residents, making it the third-largest city in Washington State. The city has a diverse population, with a rich cultural heritage and a strong sense of community.

Economic Information

Tacoma's economy is diverse, with major industries including healthcare, manufacturing, and logistics. The Port of Tacoma is a significant economic driver, facilitating international trade. The median household income in Tacoma is $59,658, and the unemployment rate is around 4.8%.

Geographic Information

Located on the shores of Puget Sound, Tacoma offers stunning waterfront views and a moderate climate with mild, wet winters and warm, dry summers. The city's location makes it a gateway to outdoor adventures in the nearby Cascade and Olympic Mountains.
