City Information

Demographic Information

Seattle is home to approximately 744,000 people, making it the largest city in Washington State. The city boasts a diverse and young population, with vibrant Asian, African American, and Hispanic communities enriching its cultural landscape.

Economic Information

Seattle's economy is a powerhouse driven by technology, aerospace, and manufacturing sectors. The city is home to global giants like Amazon and Boeing, contributing to a low unemployment rate of about 3.7% and a high median household income of $93,481.

Geographic Information

Nestled between the Puget Sound and Lake Washington, Seattle is known for its stunning natural beauty, surrounded by water, mountains, and evergreen forests. The city experiences a temperate marine climate with mild, wet winters and warm, dry summers, perfect for exploring landmarks like the Space Needle and Pike Place Market.
